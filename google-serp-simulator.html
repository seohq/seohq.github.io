HOW TO USE THIS TOOL

Optimizing your presence on the Search Engine Results Page (SERP) is critical for Click-Through Rate (CTR). Follow these steps:

  1. Enter your Meta Title: Keep it under 60 characters to avoid truncation.
  2. Write a Meta Description: Aim for 155-160 characters to summarize your content.
  3. Toggle Rich Snippets: Add ratings or dates to see how they impact visual space.
  4. Analyze the Competition: Compare your preview against top-ranking competitors.

SERP OPTIMIZATION IN 2026

In the era of AI-driven search, your meta tags serve as the "API" for LLMs and search crawlers. While Google often rewrites titles, providing a clear, entity-focused title increases your chances of maintaining brand voice.

Key Metrics:

  • Title Width: ~580 pixels (Desktop).
  • Description Width: ~990 pixels (Desktop).
  • Entities: Include primary keywords near the start of the title.
