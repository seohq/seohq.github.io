Key Features & Technical Breakthroughs

Google DeepMind built Gemma 4 on top of Gemini 3 research with four versatile sizes: Effective 2B (E2B), Effective 4B (E4B), 26B MoE (A4B), and 31B Dense:

  • Multimodal understanding — text, images (any resolution/aspect), video frame analysis, and native audio on E2B/E4B
  • Agentic capabilities — native function calling, tool use, and configurable “thinking” mode for step-by-step reasoning
  • Long context up to 256K tokens (128K on edge models) with hybrid attention for speed + depth
  • Multilingual support for 140+ languages out of the box
  • Optimized for on-device and single-GPU deployment while delivering frontier-level benchmarks
